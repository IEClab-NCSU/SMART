The cell is the fundamental
					unit of life. It is the first level of organization that presents all the
					properties of life. In "The
						Cell" unit, you learned about the different structures that
					make up a cell. Implicit in that knowledge was the notion of cells as
					continuously changing and dynamic entities; cells: grow, rebuild, repair,
						reproduce, and react to the external environment. All of these
					functions require energy.
