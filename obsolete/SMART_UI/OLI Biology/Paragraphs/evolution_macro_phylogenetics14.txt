Homologous features are the key sources of information for the construction of phylogenetic trees. A phylogenetic tree, or phylogeny, is a
            diagram that shows how a biological lineage may have branched and formed clades over
            time. A tree diagram is a hypothesis about how different species are related and is
            subject to change as more data are gathered and as analysis techniques improve. To get a
